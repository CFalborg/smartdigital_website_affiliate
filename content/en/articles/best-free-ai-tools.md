{{< faq >}}
### Which AI tool is best for beginners?
ChatGPT is the best starting point for most people. It has a good free plan, is easy to use, and can be used for almost anything. Try it for a week and see what you can use it for.

### Are free AI tools good enough?
Yes, for many purposes the free plans are more than sufficient. ChatGPT, Claude and Perplexity all have strong free plans. Only upgrade if you hit concrete limitations.

### Can AI tools be used in English?
Yes! ChatGPT, Claude and most major AI tools are excellent in English. You can write to them in English and ask them to reply in English.

### What do AI tools typically cost?
Most premium AI tools cost $10–20 per month. Many have free plans that cover casual use. See the list above for specific prices.
